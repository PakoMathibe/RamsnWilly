OUR SERVICES

WHAT WE DO

Rams & Willy Trading Project are a team of fully professional electricians offering a vast array of electrical services throughout the South Africa. All of our highly professional and experienced electrical contractors are  trained to deal with all your domestic and commercial electrical requirements.

LIGHTING UPGRADES

Upgrading a lighting system can reduce energy consumption in two ways. We can either reduce the lighting system's input wattage or reduce its hours of operation.

WIRING & INSTALL

We install electrical wiring of cabling and associated devices such as switches, distribution boards, sockets and light fittings in a structure.

  SURGE PROTECTION

We install surge protectors  devices that have been designed to protect electrical devices from voltage spikes.

GENERATOR REPAIR

SOLAR SYSTEM

Solar panels are devices that convert light into electricity. We install these devices which will enable you to use less electricity.
